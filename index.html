Who is Eva G?

Hi, I'm Eva Gierczynska, I'm Polish and have been living in France for 3 years now. I got my Master's Degree in Cultural management a couple of months ago, but because of pandemic I obviously can't find a job. I know Adobe, especially InDesign and Illustrator, but also Canva and the basics of HTML and CSS, which you can see by looking at this website that I did myself. Currently, I'm writing articles for contentyze.com. I'm smart and would like to start working with graphic design and digital art. If you would like to do a project together or you have a job offer that I may find interesting, hit me up. If you would like to see what I've already done, click here to see my portfolio.
My Little Diary is a project invented and realised entirely by me and almost everything you can see here has been done by me. It's a kind of an artistic experiment that I hope will bring its expected results.
